Privacy Policy

App Name: TextMate

Developer: Jamal Ahmed

Contact Email: jamal@gmail.com

1. Overview

TextMate is a text utility app offering AI-enhanced features such as translation, summarization, and grammar correction. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user data.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect or store any personal data. All features run client-side or use secure third-party APIs to process input without tracking or storing user information.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service:

  • OpenAI API – Used for AI features like grammar correction, summarization, and "Ask AI". Data sent to OpenAI is subject to their privacy policy.

4. Advertising

This app does not display ads and does not use ad networks such as AdMob or Facebook Audience Network.

5. User Accounts

TextMate does not require users to sign up or log in. No authentication or user tracking is implemented.

6. Data Storage

Any user data (like temporary text input) is stored locally on your device and is not accessible by us or any external party. Deleting the app or clearing its cache will remove all locally stored data.

7. Permissions

The app may request access to the following:

  • Internet – To access AI and translation services via the OpenAI API.
  • Microphone – For the Speech-to-Text feature. (Please note: This feature is not available in the current version; it will be added in the next version.)

8. Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

9. Location Access

TextMate does not access or use your device's location.

10. Data Retention

No personal data is collected, so there is no data retention beyond temporary local storage on your device.

11. User Rights

You have full control over your data. To remove any stored data, simply uninstall the app or clear its data via device settings.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with a revised date.

13. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: jamal@gmail.com
